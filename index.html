Kido Summer Camp 2021

Thank you for your interest in our Summer Camp which will run for 4 weeks from 19th July - 13th August for children aged 2 - 6 years old.

Each week there will be bilingual (English / Mandarin) activities such as Literacy, Arts & Crafts, Maths, Science, Technology and Physical Development Activities.

Morning half day sessions are available from 9am - 12pm. The fee for half day session is HK$2,750 per week. Minimum of 2 weeks enrollment required.

Please complete the form below to register your interest. Once we have received these details we will contact you to confirm your space. Should you have any questions, please contact us at infohk@kido.school or 2177 0001.

Terms and Conditions

All closures or class suspension relating but not limited to weather, health, political stability and COVID are dictated by the EDB and/or associated government authorities and must be followed by all schools. In the case that schools are suspended for longer than 2 consecutive days, all classes will move to online virtual sessions. For each day that classes are moved online, you will receive HK$100. For those students enrolled with Kido in the 21-22 academic year, this will be credited to your first months tuition fee payment. For those who are not attending in the 21-22 academic year, this will be refunded to you after the summer camp. No make-up classes will be arranged under any circumstances for these or student absences.

Fees are non refundable and non transferable. Your space is only confirmed upon confirmation of payment.

All parents/guardians must complete a Travel & Health Declaration form prior to attendance. Children may not attend school if they are suffering from sickness or display any illness symptoms including but not limited to a temperature above 37.5. coughing, running nose, vomiting and diarrhea.
